## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests
- Triage and respond to issues
- Manage releases and versioning
- Enforce code quality and project standards
- Maintain documentation and community health

## Becoming a Maintainer

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for how to contribute. Consistent, high-quality contributions over time may lead to a maintainer invitation.
